---
title: Driver can't log in
description: Fix driver login issues
---

Check these in order:

1. Go to **Team** and confirm the driver's email is correct
2. Click **Resend invite** to send a fresh login link
3. Ask the driver to check their spam folder
4. Confirm the driver is logging in at **towvio.com/driver/login** not the main login page
5. Check that your plan has available seats — Starter is limited to 3 seats

## Seat limit reached

If your plan is full:

- Go to **Billing → Upgrade plan** to add more seats
- Or deactivate an existing team member under **Team** to free up a seat

## Driver forgot password

1. Go to **towvio.com/driver/login**
2. Click **Forgot password**
3. Enter their email
4. They'll receive a reset link within a few minutes

## Still not working?

Contact support at support@towvio.com with the driver's name and email address.
