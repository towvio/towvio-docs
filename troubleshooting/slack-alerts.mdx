---
title: Slack alerts not posting
description: Fix Slack notifications that aren't appearing in your channel
---

Check these in order:

1. Go to **Settings → Slack** and confirm your workspace shows as **Active**
2. Click **Send test** — if the test message posts, the connection is working
3. Check that the notification type is toggled on — Pro/Elite required for call summaries and digest
4. Confirm the Towvio bot has permission to post to the selected channel in Slack
5. If the workspace shows as disconnected, click **Add to Slack** to reconnect
