---
title: Job didn't get created after a call
description: Steps to take when an AI dispatcher call doesn't create a job
---

Check these in order:

1. **Did the call last at least 60 seconds?** Very short calls may not have enough data to create a job.
2. **Did the caller provide a name, phone, or location?** Towvio requires at least one of these to create a job.
3. **Is your Vapi assistant ID configured correctly?** Go to **Settings → AI Dispatcher** and confirm the assistant ID matches your Vapi account.
4. **Check the call in your Vapi dashboard** — look for the end-of-call-report webhook firing.
5. **Check your jobs dashboard** — filter by source "AI Dispatcher" to see if it was created but is hard to find.

## Still not working?

Contact support with the following information:

- Call date and time
- Caller phone number
- Your dispatch number that was called

Email: support@towvio.com
