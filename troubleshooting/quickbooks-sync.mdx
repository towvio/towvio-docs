---
title: Invoice not syncing to QuickBooks
description: Fix Towvio to QuickBooks sync issues by checking the connection, job completion status, 15-minute sync window, and handling duplicate invoices.
---

Check these in order:

1. Confirm QuickBooks is connected under **Settings → Integrations → QuickBooks**
2. Confirm the job status is **Completed** — only completed jobs sync
3. QuickBooks sync runs every 15 minutes — wait and refresh
4. If the customer doesn't exist in QuickBooks, Towvio will create them automatically on first sync
5. Disconnect and reconnect QuickBooks if syncs have stopped entirely

## Reconnecting QuickBooks

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → QuickBooks**
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Click **Connect QuickBooks**
4. Log in and authorize again
5. Syncs will resume within 15 minutes

## Duplicate invoices

Towvio uses the job confirmation number to prevent duplicate invoices. If you see duplicates in QuickBooks, contact support at support@towvio.com with the job confirmation number.
