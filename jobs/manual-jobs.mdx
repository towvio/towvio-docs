---
title: Manually creating a job
description: Create a job directly from the dashboard
---

1. Go to **Jobs → New job**
2. Enter the customer name and phone
3. Select service type
4. Enter pickup address and dropoff address (if applicable)
5. Enter vehicle details
6. Click **Create job**

The customer will receive an SMS confirmation automatically.

## When to create jobs manually

- Walk-in or in-person requests
- Jobs called in directly to your personal phone
- Jobs transferred from another provider
- Test jobs for training purposes

## Tip

If you have a repeat customer, search their phone number first — Towvio will pre-fill their name and vehicle details from previous jobs.
