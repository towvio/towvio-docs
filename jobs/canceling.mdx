---
title: Canceling a job
description: Cancel an active Towvio job from the dashboard, notify the assigned driver and customer, and learn how canceled jobs affect your monthly limit.
---

1. Open the job from the **Jobs** dashboard
2. Click **Cancel job**
3. Select a reason
4. The job is marked canceled and removed from active dispatch

## What happens after cancellation

- The customer receives an SMS notification
- The job is marked canceled in your dashboard
- The job still counts toward your monthly job limit
- The job remains in your history for reporting purposes

## Common cancellation reasons

- Customer called back and no longer needs service
- Duplicate job created
- Customer unreachable
- Outside service area
