---
title: Managing job status
description: Track jobs from dispatch to completion
---

Jobs move through these statuses:

**Pending → Dispatched → En route → On scene → Completed**

## Updating a job status

1. Go to **Jobs** and click the job
2. Click the status button to advance it
3. Status changes are logged automatically with a timestamp

## Status definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pending | Job created, not yet assigned |
| Dispatched | Driver or contractor assigned |
| En route | Driver is heading to pickup location |
| On scene | Driver has arrived |
| Completed | Job finished, ready to invoice |
| Canceled | Job was canceled |

## Job status log

Every status change is recorded with a timestamp and visible in the job detail view. This gives you a full audit trail for every job.
