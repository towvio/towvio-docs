Jobs in Towvio are created three ways:

| Source | How |
|---|---|
| AI dispatcher | Automatically after every inbound call |
| Manual | You create it from the Jobs dashboard |
| Vapi webhook | Triggered by a completed call event |

Every job gets a unique 9-digit confirmation number automatically.

## Job fields

Every job captures:

- Customer name and phone
- Vehicle year, make, model, color
- Service type
- Pickup and dropoff address
- EV, AWD, neutral, and keys status
- Payment status
- Tracking token for customer self-tracking
