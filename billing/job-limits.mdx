---
title: Job limit behavior
description: What happens when you approach or hit your monthly job limit
---

## Warning at 80%

When you reach 80% of your monthly job limit, Towvio will:

- Show a warning banner in your dashboard
- Send you an email notification
- If Slack is connected, post an alert to your channel (Pro/Elite)

## At the limit

When you reach 100% of your monthly job limit:

- New jobs cannot be created
- Your AI dispatcher will still answer calls but jobs won't be logged
- Manual job creation is also blocked
- You'll see an error in your dashboard

## How to fix it

- **Upgrade your plan** — go to **Billing → Upgrade plan**. New limits are active immediately.
- **Wait for reset** — limits reset at the start of your next billing cycle

## Checking your usage

Go to **Billing** to see your current job count, limit, and days remaining in your billing cycle.
