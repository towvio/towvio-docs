If you exceed your included AI minutes, Towvio charges a per-minute overage rate:

| Plan | Overage rate |
|---|---|
| Starter | $0.24/min |
| Pro | $0.22/min |
| Elite | $0.20/min |
| Enterprise | $0.18/min |

## How overages are calculated

- Overages are calculated nightly
- Charged automatically to your card on file
- You'll receive an email receipt for each overage charge

## How to monitor usage

- Go to **ROI Dashboard** to see AI minutes used vs included in real time
- If Slack is connected, overage alerts post to your channel (Pro/Elite)

## How to reduce overages

- Upgrade to a higher plan for more included minutes
- Review call history for unusually long calls
- Shorter calls mean fewer minutes used — Trevor is optimized to complete intake in under 3 minutes
