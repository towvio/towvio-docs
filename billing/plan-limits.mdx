description: Jobs, AI minutes, and seats by plan

Each plan includes a monthly job limit, AI minute limit, and seat limit:

| Plan | Jobs/month | AI minutes/month | Seats |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | 200 | 200 | 3 |
| Pro | 500 | 600 | 25 |
| Elite | 1,200 | 1,500 | 50 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |

Limits reset at the start of each billing cycle.

## What counts toward your job limit

- Every job created — by AI dispatcher, manually, or via webhook
- Canceled jobs still count
- Test jobs count

## What counts toward your AI minute limit

- Every inbound call handled by your AI dispatcher
- Minutes are rounded up to the nearest minute per call

## What counts toward your seat limit

- Every active team member with a Towvio login
- Drivers and managers both count as seats
- Contractors do not count as seats
