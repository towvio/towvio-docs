---
title: Upgrading your plan
description: Upgrade your Towvio plan from Starter, Pro, or Elite to unlock more jobs, AI minutes, and seats, with prorated billing applied immediately.
---

1. Go to **Billing** in the left navigation
2. Click **Upgrade plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm — your new limits are active immediately

## What happens when you upgrade

- Job and AI minute limits increase immediately
- You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle
- Your next full charge is at your new plan rate

## Plan options

| Plan | Price | Jobs | AI minutes | Seats |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $99/mo | 200 | 200 | 3 |
| Pro | $249/mo | 500 | 600 | 25 |
| Elite | $449/mo | 1,200 | 1,500 | 50 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |

## Downgrading

To downgrade your plan contact support at support@towvio.com. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
