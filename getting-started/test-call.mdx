---
title: Making your first test call
description: Verify your AI dispatcher is working correctly
---

1. Find your dispatch number under **Settings → AI Dispatcher**
2. Call the number from any phone
3. Walk through the intake — give a name, location, and service type
4. Hang up and check your **Jobs** dashboard — the job should appear within 30 seconds

## What to expect

- The AI will greet you and ask for your information
- It will confirm your location and service type
- After the call ends, a job is created automatically
- You'll see it in your dashboard with status **Pending**

## Job didn't appear?

See [Job didn't get created after a call](/troubleshooting/job-not-created) for next steps.
