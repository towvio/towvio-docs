---
title: Adding drivers and trucks
description: Get your team and fleet set up in Towvio
---

## Adding a driver

1. Go to **Team → Add driver**
2. Enter their name, phone, and email
3. Assign their role (Driver or Manager)
4. They'll receive a login link by email

## Adding a truck

1. Go to **Trucks → Add truck**
2. Enter the truck type (flatbed, wheel lift, rotator, etc.)
3. Enter capacity and any notes
4. Assign a driver if needed
