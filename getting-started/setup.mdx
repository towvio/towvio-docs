---
title: Setting up your account
description: Create your Towvio account, pick a plan, configure business settings like timezone and phone number, and prepare your org for dispatching.
---

1. Go to **towvio.com/signup** and create your account.
2. Select your plan — Starter, Pro, or Elite.
3. Enter your billing information to activate your subscription.
4. You'll land on your dashboard. Your org is created and ready.

## First things to do after signup

- Add your business name and timezone under **Settings → General**
- Add your business phone number under **Settings → Notifications**
- Add at least one truck under **Trucks**
- Connect your AI dispatcher under **Settings → AI Dispatcher**
