---
title: Connecting your AI dispatcher
description: Set up Trevor or another persona to handle inbound calls automatically
---

Towvio's AI dispatcher answers inbound calls automatically and creates jobs without you lifting a finger.

1. Go to **Settings → AI Dispatcher**
2. Select your persona — Trevor, Brianna, Cole, Rosa, Troy, or Dana
3. Your inbound dispatch number is automatically provisioned
4. Call the number to run a test — a job should appear in your dashboard within seconds

## What the AI dispatcher collects on every call

- Caller name and phone number
- Vehicle year, make, model, and color
- Service needed (tow, lockout, jump start, tire change, fuel delivery, winch out)
- Pickup location
- Whether the vehicle is EV, AWD, in neutral, and has keys
