---
title: Viewing call history
description: Review past AI dispatcher calls and transcripts
---

1. Go to **ROI Dashboard** in the left navigation
2. Scroll to the **Call history** section
3. Filter by date range, persona, or disposition
4. Click any call to see the full transcript and job created

## What you can see per call

- Call date and time
- Caller phone number
- Duration in minutes
- Persona that handled the call
- Disposition (completed, hung up, no answer)
- Full transcript
- Job that was created from the call

## AI minutes usage

Every call consumes AI minutes from your plan. You can monitor your usage in real time on the **ROI Dashboard**.

- Starter: 200 minutes/month
- Pro: 600 minutes/month
- Elite: 1,500 minutes/month

Overage charges apply if you exceed your included minutes. See [AI minute overages](/billing/overages) for details.
