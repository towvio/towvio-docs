Towvio's AI dispatcher answers inbound calls automatically — 24/7, no hold time, no missed calls.

## The 10-question intake

Every call follows the same structured intake:

1. Caller name
2. Callback number
3. Vehicle year
4. Vehicle make and model
5. Vehicle color
6. Service needed
7. Pickup location
8. Dropoff location (if tow)
9. Is the vehicle EV?
10. Does the customer have the keys?

## Supported service types

- Standard tow
- Flatbed tow
- EV tow
- Lockout
- Jump start
- Tire change
- Fuel delivery
- Winch out
- Long distance tow

## Languages supported

Trevor is multilingual and will detect and respond in the caller's language automatically.
