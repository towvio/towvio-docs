Towvio offers six AI dispatcher personas. Each has a distinct voice and communication style but follows the same intake script.

## Available personas

| Persona | Style |
|---|---|
| Trevor | Deep, authoritative, neutral American. Default. |
| Brianna | Warm, friendly, conversational |
| Cole | Calm, professional, efficient |
| Rosa | Bilingual, empathetic, detail-oriented |
| Troy | Direct, confident, no-nonsense |
| Dana | Upbeat, reassuring, customer-focused |

## Changing your persona

1. Go to **Settings → AI Dispatcher**
2. Click **Change persona**
3. Select your preferred persona
4. Save — the new persona is active immediately

## Tips

- Trevor and Cole work well for professional fleets
- Brianna and Dana work well for consumer-facing operations
- Rosa is ideal if you serve a Spanish-speaking customer base
