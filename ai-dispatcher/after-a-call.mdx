---
title: What happens after a call
description: How Towvio processes a call and creates a job
---

1. Call ends
2. Vapi sends a webhook to Towvio
3. Towvio extracts job data from the call transcript
4. Job is created in your dashboard with status **Pending**
5. Customer receives SMS confirmation
6. If Slack is connected, a call summary posts to your channel

This happens within 30 seconds of the call ending.

## What gets extracted from every call

- Customer name and phone number
- Vehicle year, make, model, and color
- Service type
- Pickup and dropoff address
- EV, AWD, neutral, and keys status
- Any additional notes from the conversation
