---
title: QuickBooks sync
description: Automatically sync completed jobs and invoices to QuickBooks
---

Towvio syncs completed jobs and invoices to QuickBooks automatically.

## How to connect

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → QuickBooks**
2. Click **Connect QuickBooks**
3. Log in to your QuickBooks account and authorize
4. Completed jobs sync within 15 minutes

## What syncs

- Customer records
- Completed job invoices
- Payment status

## Sync frequency

- Syncs run every 15 minutes automatically
- You can trigger a manual sync from **Settings → Integrations → QuickBooks**

## Notes

- Only jobs with status **Completed** sync to QuickBooks
- If a customer doesn't exist in QuickBooks, Towvio creates them automatically
- Duplicate invoices are prevented by job confirmation number

## Disconnecting

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → QuickBooks**
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Existing synced data remains in QuickBooks
