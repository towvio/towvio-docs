---
title: Connecting Slack
description: Get job alerts and daily digests in your Slack workspace
---

Slack integration lets your team receive job alerts and daily digests without logging into Towvio.

## How to connect

1. Go to **Settings → Slack**
2. Click **Add to Slack**
3. Authorize Towvio to post to your workspace
4. Select which channel to post to
5. Toggle which notifications you want
6. Click **Save settings**

## Available notifications by plan

| Notification | Starter | Pro | Elite |
|---|---|---|---|
| New job alert | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| AI call summary | No | Yes | Yes |
| Daily digest (6am) | No | Yes | Yes |
| Overage alerts | No | Yes | Yes |

## Sending a test message

1. Go to **Settings → Slack**
2. Select your channel
3. Click **Send test**
4. Check your Slack channel for the test message

## Disconnecting

1. Go to **Settings → Slack**
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Towvio will stop posting to your workspace immediately
