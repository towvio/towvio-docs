Connect your Google Business Profile to monitor and reply to reviews directly from Towvio.

## How to connect

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Google Business Profile**
2. Click **Connect Google**
3. Authorize Towvio to access your profile
4. Reviews appear in your dashboard automatically

## What you can do

- View all Google reviews in one place
- Reply to reviews directly from Towvio
- Monitor your average rating over time
- Get notified of new reviews

## Tips for responding to reviews

- Respond to every review — positive and negative
- Keep responses professional and brief
- Thank customers for positive reviews
- For negative reviews acknowledge the issue and offer to resolve it offline

## Disconnecting

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Google Business Profile**
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Existing review data remains in your dashboard
